About:
This application is created as part of a master project at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU). The application is a toolkit used for evaluation purposes, mostly designed for experiments conducted within the field of Extended reality experiments. Its main goal is to make the total evaluation process more efficient and easier to conduct, as well as having a common place to store your experimental data.
